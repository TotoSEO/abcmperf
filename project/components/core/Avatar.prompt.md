Round avatar with image or auto-initials fallback. Use for team members, testimonials, account menus.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Caroline Meyer" size="lg" />
<Avatar src="/team/kevin.jpg" name="Kevin Kanel" ring />
```

Props: `src`, `name` (drives initials), `size` (`sm|md|lg|xl`), `ring` (yellow accent ring), `hue` (logo-palette tint on the fallback).
