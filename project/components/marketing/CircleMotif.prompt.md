The signature ABCM overlapping-circle device — a decorative brand accent for heroes, section corners, and dividers.

```jsx
<CircleMotif blend="screen" size={160} />           {/* on a night-blue section */}
<CircleMotif blend="multiply" size={90} count={3} /> {/* on white */}
```

Props: `size` (px), `overlap` (0–0.6), `blend` (`screen` on dark / `multiply` on light), `opacity`, `count` (1–5). Decorative only (aria-hidden).
