Big-number statistic for "key figures" rows. Prefix/suffix render in the yellow accent.

```jsx
<StatCard prefix="+" value="150" suffix="" label="projets livrés" />
<StatCard value="2015" label="depuis" />
<StatCard value="9" label="experts dédiés" />
```

Props: `value`, `prefix`, `suffix` (accent-coloured), `label`. Works on dark sections.
